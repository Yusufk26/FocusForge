Focus Forge

Instructions

The goal of Focus Forge is to click the target with your mouse before it moves to increase your score because failing to do so will result in you losing a life. Aim correctly because if you miss the target you will lose another life. The game becomes harder as your score rises, but you are rewarded with a study tip about ADHD. Press "back" and "play" to begin playing! Enjoy achieving those goals!
